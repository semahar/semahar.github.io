About

The Westin St. Francis San Francisco is a historic luxury hotel and wedding venue in California, known for its charming ambiance, ornate decor, and magnificent clock.

Facilities and Capacity:

The Westin St. Francis boasts 33 elegant and unique event spaces with stunning views, lavish decor, and historical charm, including Victor's, Alexandra's, and The Colonial Ballroom.

Services Offered:

The Westin St. Francis San Francisco is a stunning wedding venue in Union Square with personalized event services, offering both intimate and grand celebrations for an unforgettable wedding experience.

  • Accommodations
  • Setup and Cleanup
  • Bridal suite
  • Event planning
  • Event rentals
  • Lighting/sound
  • Liability insurance
  • Cake
Cuisine

The highly experienced culinary staff at The Westin St. Francis is led by an award-winning team of chefs. They offer an array of custom-designed gourmet menus, from an all-chocolate dessert buffet to wine pairings.

Accommodation

The Westin St. Francis provides modern amenities, including the revolutionary “Heavenly Bed,” the Starwood Preferred Guest rewards program and dedicated and attentive staff. Their timeless yet modern rooms are favorites of guests looking for a relaxing stay near San Francisco’s entertainment and attractions.

Other Services

The Westin St. Francis is an ideal location for hosting private parties of varying sizes. In addition to wedding ceremonies and receptions, this location can accommodate all of your wedding events, such as engagement parties, bridal showers and rehearsal dinners. This venue is also available for hosting corporate meetings, holiday parties, birthdays, anniversaries and other special occasions.

Location

The Westin St. Francis is the only hotel located on San Francisco’s Union Square, a popular gathering place for generations of travelers. This excellent location in the heart of San Francisco is just minutes from San Franscisco’s most popular entertainment venues and attractions.
